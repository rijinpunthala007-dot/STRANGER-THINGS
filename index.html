STRANGER THINGS

THE INTERACTIVE CHRONICLE

The Upside Down

November 1983. Will Byers vanishes into the Upside Down. A telekinetic girl named Eleven arrives. Friends unite to fight the Demogorgon and uncover a government conspiracy.

The Mind Flayer

October 1984. The Mind Flayer looms over Hawkins in a storm of shadows. Will is possessed by the hive mind. Eleven returns to close the gate before the darkness takes over.

Starcourt Mall

Summer 1985. The Starcourt Mall opens, hiding a secret Russian base beneath. The Mind Flayer returns to build a physical body. Billy is lost. The Battle of Starcourt begins.

Vecna's Curse

Spring 1986. A new horror named Vecna strikes from the Upside Down, targeting trauma. The gang splits between Hawkins, California, and Russia. Max runs up that hill. War is coming.
